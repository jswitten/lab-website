        The Witten Lab is recruiting motivated researchers at all levels to join our team at Case Western Reserve University starting June 2026.

        We are seeking people with interest in any of:

        - Artificial intelligence
        - Nanoparticle formulation and characterization
        - Gene therapy or nucleic acid delivery
        - High-throughput experimentation
        - Molecular biology
        - Synthetic chemistry
        - Airway biology
        - Regenerative medicine
        - Virology

        ## Graduate Students

        PhD students join the lab through the [MSTP](https://case.edu/medicine/mstp/) or [BSTP](https://case.edu/medicine/bstp/) programs at Case Western Reserve University. Prospective students should apply to one of these programs and indicate interest in the Witten Lab.

        ---

        ## Postdoctoral Researchers

        Interested candidates should email Dr. Witten with:
        1. CV
        2. Brief description of research interests and why you're interested in the lab
        3. Names of 2-3 references
